Crawl4AI Product Crawler

Enter an e‑commerce homepage. The app will discover up to 30 internal pages, extract products (name, price, image, description) using JSON CSS selectors (no LLM), and let you download a CSV.

Must be a public page you have permission to crawl.
Uses crawl4ai with JsonCssExtractionStrategy and multi‑URL crawling (no LLM, schema‑based extraction).
